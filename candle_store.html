How to Add Your Own Images:

  1. Find an image online or on your computer
  2. Upload it to an image hosting service (like Imgur, Google Photos, etc.)
  3. Right-click on the image and select "Copy image address"
  4. Replace the src attribute in the img tag with your image URL
  5. Save the file and refresh your browser to see the change

Example: Change src="https://example.com/your-image.jpg"
